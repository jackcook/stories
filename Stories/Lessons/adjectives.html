Adjectives


In English, you put adjectives before the noun they describe. However, most French adjectives are placed after the noun they describe. So you'd say "un sac vert" (a green bag) or "une maison bleue" (a blue house).
However, it's important to know that there are a few exceptions. Adjectives that express beauty, age, number, goodness, size) are placed before the noun they describe.
